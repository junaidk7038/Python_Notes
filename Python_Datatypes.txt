Collection:

list: 

Lists are versatile and commonly used to store an ordered collection of items.
Lists are suitable for situations where you need to maintain a dynamic collection of items,
 such as a list of user names, a shopping cart, or a log of events.

Tupples: it is a constant list,we cant change it.

Tuples are useful when you have a collection of related data that should remain unchanged
Ex: addahar card ,pan card , anything that will not change,

Set:Sets are used to store a collection of unique elements.

such as unique user IDs, unique email addresses, or unique keywords.
Characteristics: Sets do not allow duplicate elements, and the order of elements is not guaranteed.
